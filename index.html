Who We Are

The Wallace Investment Group, LLC (TWIG) is a forward-thinking parent investment and holding company based in Fairfax, Virginia. We specialize in identifying and nurturing profitable ventures across multiple sectors.

Founded on principles of integrity, innovation, and sustainable growth, we build and maintain a diverse portfolio of businesses and investments designed to create lasting value for our stakeholders.

Our approach combines traditional investment wisdom with modern entrepreneurial thinking, allowing us to adapt to changing markets while maintaining a steady focus on long-term success.
